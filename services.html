Four ways we get involved

Pick a single engagement, or bring us in for the full lifecycle — discovery through go-live and beyond.

01

Business Central implementation

Full-cycle Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central rollouts, run with GAP-FIT rigor so scope is grounded in what your operation actually needs — not a generic template applied and hoped-for.

Most failed BC implementations don't fail on technology — they fail because nobody scored what standard functionality could handle versus what genuinely needed customization, so budgets get consumed building things that were already available out of the box, or worse, standard features get forced onto processes they were never meant to fit.

Typical duration10–20 weeks
Team1–3 consultants
MethodologySure Step-aligned
What's included
  • Current-state process mapping across finance, inventory, sales, and purchasing
  • GAP-FIT scoring — every requirement rated against standard BC functionality before any custom work is scoped
  • Chart of accounts, dimensions, and financial reporting structure design
  • Inventory costing method selection (FIFO, LIFO, average, standard, specific) matched to how you actually value stock
  • Approval workflows, number series, and role-based security setup
  • Data migration from legacy ERP, Tally, or spreadsheets — including historical transaction history where needed
  • UAT cycles run with your actual operators, not just IT sign-off
  • Go-live cutover planning with a rollback path, plus hypercare support for the first 2–4 weeks post-launch
Deliverables
  • GAP-FIT document with cost/effort attached to every identified gap
  • Configured BC environment (sandbox and production)
  • Migrated and validated master and transactional data
  • End-user training materials and recorded walkthroughs
  • Go-live runbook and post-launch support plan
02

Dynamics 365 CRM

Sales, service, and customer engagement workflows tuned to how your team actually sells and supports — the CRM adapts to your sales cycle, not the other way around.

Most CRM rollouts get treated as a database of contacts. The value is in the workflow: what happens automatically when a lead moves stages, what a service agent sees the moment a case is logged, what data flows back into Business Central without anyone re-typing it.

Typical duration6–12 weeks
Team1–2 consultants
Often paired withBC integration
What's included
  • Sales pipeline and opportunity stage design, matched to your actual sales cycle length and decision points
  • Lead scoring and assignment rules
  • Customer service case management with SLA timers and escalation paths
  • Quote-to-order workflow, including approval thresholds
  • Marketing list segmentation and campaign tracking
  • Integration with Business Central for a single customer and order view — no duplicate data entry between systems
  • Dashboards for pipeline visibility and service-level reporting
Deliverables
  • Configured CRM environment with custom entities where needed
  • BC↔CRM integration (real-time or scheduled sync, depending on volume)
  • Sales and service team training
  • Reporting dashboards for leadership visibility
03

AI & GenAI enablement

Copilot extensions, custom AI agents, and automation layered onto your existing ERP and CRM stack — built to reduce manual work in specific, measurable places, not added as a generic "AI feature" nobody ends up using.

We scope this the same way we scope ERP work: identify the specific manual task costing the most hours (invoice matching, PO data entry, report compilation), then build the narrowest automation that solves it reliably.

Typical duration4–8 weeks per use case
Team1 consultant + dev
Runs onAzure AI / Copilot Studio
What's included
  • Microsoft Copilot customization for Business Central and CRM — surfacing the specific data your team actually queries
  • Custom AI agents for document processing (invoice/PO extraction, three-way matching)
  • Natural-language reporting interfaces on top of BC financial and operational data
  • Workflow automation triggered across systems (e.g., a CRM case auto-creating a BC service order)
  • Evaluation of accuracy and failure modes before anything goes into production use
Deliverables
  • Working AI agent or Copilot extension, tested against real historical data
  • Documentation of what the system handles automatically vs. what still needs human review
  • Handover training for whoever owns the workflow day to day
04

Custom solutions

Integrations, legacy migrations, and bespoke builds for the gaps that off-the-shelf configuration can't close — scoped only after GAP-FIT confirms standard functionality genuinely won't cover it.

Typical durationVaries by scope
Team1–2 developers
Built onAL / Business Central extensions
What's included
  • EDI integration for retailer/distributor order and invoice exchange
  • E-commerce platform integration (Shopify, WooCommerce, custom storefronts) with BC inventory and order sync
  • Third-party API integrations (logistics, payment gateways, banking)
  • Legacy system data migration and cleansing, including historical reporting continuity
  • Custom AL extensions for functionality genuinely outside standard BC scope
  • Reporting and analytics builds beyond standard Power BI templates
Deliverables
  • Scoped technical design document before development starts
  • Tested integration or extension, deployed to production
  • Source code and documentation handed over — no vendor lock-in
